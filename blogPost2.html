In today's blog I looked into the various types of animations you can create using CSS,
what you can create animations for, and what you can do with animations. The main properties of
animations include: transformations, transitions, and keyframes. Transformations relate to the movement,
the size, location, and other things which dont change but alter the objects appearance. Transitions are
all about how well you can perform the transition smoothly. Keyframes are the exact timing you want to change an
animation at a given time or state.

for individuals to have tons of options to create a nice GUI and enhance user experience. Making 3D effects and
interactive details part of your web design strategy can differentiate your site from competitors.
