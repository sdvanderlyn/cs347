Chatbots

Chatbots are a computer program designed to simulate conversation with human users, and todays blog focused on over the internet.
Early chatbots started out with lists of different expected inputs from users and pre-selected answers to be outputted. Chatbots were
created to take human interactation out of repetitively asked questions and actions done in fields like customer service and tasks to prevent
queues forming on hot lines and chat support. With more advances, chatbots are able to access systems and databases to provide even further
assistance to companies and organizations to reach clients needs at a faster rate.

Today, chatbots are being equipped with artificial inteligence to make more realistic forms of life. The is done by a predictive model software
where the inputed text, voices, and writings are transformed into the most relevant and applicable continuation there is to produce an output that
is close to human interaction. On the business side of things, this ability to quickly take in writing and other inputs will pave the way for faster
contractual paperwork and documentation in multi-langual environments.
